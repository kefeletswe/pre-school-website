About Kindergarten Kids

We offer a home from home experience for children. Our experienced staff believes each child should be treated with love and respect. Children learn best when they feel safe and secure. Busy children are happy children. We cater for children from two to five years of age. We offer a combination of Montessori/Playschool. During the first part of the session we have free play. We do tabletop activities which include a worksheet and a craft each day. Children carry out many different activities in a caring environment. We have a large outside play area for the children. Each childs personality is nurtured and they are encouraged to develop their own uniqueness. During the summer term we go outside to play tennis, frisbe, foot ball and water play. Highlights of the school year include a visit from Santa, a Christmas concert, a lamb who needs to be bottle fed, chicks, ducklings and goslings come to visit. We offer the free ECCE scheme on a four day basis.
